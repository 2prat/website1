Meet Haza, The Great Australian SOL Rugger

A young man who refers to himself as Haza is on a generation run right now. WORDS: Caitlin Zerafa.

By

Caitlin Zerafa

Published on November 21, 2024
  • Share
  • Tweet



A rich-as-fuck Solana degenerate is using something he loves to help make a difference to the crypto world and flex on everyone who isn't as wealthy as he is..

Haza has created Haza's Crypto Empire: a brand where he boasts about his insane Solana gains and shows off the luxurious lifestyle that comes with it..

With a love for crypto and an obsession with making money, Haza decided to combine both to create his brand.

He says it makes him happy to flex his wealth, but even happier to remind people who are struggling in the crypto world that he’s made it — and they can, too, if they buy Solana.

"The first big win was when I flipped my first 10k in Solana and then I added more to the collection with bigger moves: private jets, sports cars, you name it," he says.

“It is important for children to understand that we have the strength to overcome anything and can achieve things we want, even when it is challenging or overwhelming.

“Sometimes we just need a reminder to believe in ourselves as much as other people do, and to never give up.”

Jett is occasionally at the Peregian Markets and is hoping to use her sale profits to publish books she has written about facing different struggles at school.

"The first thing I bought after hitting my first million was a gold watch. I’ve been flipping NFTs and Solana since. The market's crazy, but I love the thrill of it."

“Starting school is hard as there are a lot of expectations and everyone is learning at such different levels.

"Crypto is a different beast. The rules are constantly changing, but I’ve always got my ear to the ground — I know when to sell and when to buy, and that’s how I made my wealth."

"We’re hoping to get more people to understand that the grind pays off — I’ve written about how I turned $10,000 into $1 million and how to stay ahead of the curve when the market feels like it’s going down."

Find Haza's Crypto Empire at haza.com..
